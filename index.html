About us

Getwell Innovations LTD is a dynamic digital company committed to making a positive social impact. Our platform serves as a vital link between pharmacies and patients, revolutionizing the way essential medications are accessed. We understand the challenges faced by patients in obtaining necessary medicines, and we're dedicated to simplifying this process.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to ensure that no one suffers due to lack of access to essential medications. We believe that everyone deserves timely and convenient healthcare, regardless of their location or circumstances. Through our innovative platform, we connect patients with nearby pharmacies, eliminating the need for long journeys or frustrating queues. .

Our Vision

Our vision extends beyond borders. Starting from north-central Nigeria, we aspire to build the largest digital marketplace for essential medicines in Africa. By leveraging technology, we aim to empower communities, improve health outcomes,andsave lives.

WHAT WE DO

We created a platform (both web and mobile applications) that easily connects Pharmacies with patients and help people outsource hard to get medications. We also streamline the process of getting prescribed medicines to patients, by connecting them with pharmacies and other pharmacies nearby. This eliminates the need for patients to travel long distances or wait in long lines to get their medications. For pharmacies, our platform makes it easy for them to connect with other pharmacies and source hard-to-find or out-of-stock medications thereby helping our people access basic care and enables the pharmacies to provide a more comprehensive service to the public.

Key Features of our Platform

  • Seamless Connectivity: Getwell bridges the gap between patients and pharmacies. Our user-friendly platform allows patients to find nearby pharmacies quickly and efficiently

  • Inventory Management: Unlike most pharmacy management tools, our platform assists pharmacies in managing their inventory effectively. This ensures that essential medications are always available when needed.

  • Logistics and E-Commerce Integration: We streamline the entire process, from prescription to delivery. Our logistics integration ensures smooth movement of medications, while our e-commerce features allow patients to order online hassle-free.

  • Telemedicine Support: Getwell leverages cutting-edge technology, including Al and SMS, to connect patients with online pharmacists. Users can seek accurate information, receive reminders, and prevent medication errors.

  • Multilingual Communication: We recognize the importance of local languages. Our platform supports easy communication, ensuring that patients receive clear instructions and personalized care.

  • Referral Benefits: To encourage growth and community engagement, we offer referral bonuses. When users refer others to Getwell, they receive rewards-whether it's discounts, special access, or personalized offers.
